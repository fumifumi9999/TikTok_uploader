Privacy Policy

Application name: Short Video Uploader
Last updated: April 2025

1. Overview

Short Video Uploader is a desktop application that allows users to upload MP4 videos to their TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API. This Privacy Policy explains what information Short Video Uploader collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Information We Collect

Short Video Uploader stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your TikTok OAuth access token and refresh token (saved in a local .env file)
  • Your TikTok client key and client secret (entered by you and saved in .env)

Short Video Uploader does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind is performed.

3. How We Use Your Information

The credentials stored locally are used solely to authenticate requests to the TikTok API on your behalf — specifically to upload videos and publish posts to your own TikTok account.

4. Data Sharing

Short Video Uploader does not share your data with any third parties. The only external communication is directly between your device and TikTok's official API endpoints (open.tiktokapis.com).

5. Data Retention

All data is retained locally in the .env file on your own device. You can delete this file at any time to remove all stored credentials.

6. Security

Credentials are stored in a plain-text .env file on your local machine. You are responsible for securing access to your device and this file.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
